Library Policy

Rules &
Regulations

Please follow these rules to maintain a peaceful environment for all students.

01
Complete SilenceTalking, whispering, and phone calls are strictly prohibited inside the study halls. Use mobile phones only in the designated area.
02
No Food InsideEating or drinking (except water bottles) is not allowed inside the study halls. Use the refreshment area for snacks and meals.
03
Seat Booking PolicyYou cannot reserve seats for absent friends. Unoccupied reserved seats will be freed after 30 minutes without notice.
04
ID Card MandatoryMembers must carry their library ID card at all times. Entry without valid ID will be denied at the gate.
05
Electronic DevicesUse of laptops, tablets, and headphones is allowed. However, device sound must be off or use earphones at low volume.
06
CleanlinessKeep your desk and surroundings clean. Dispose waste in dustbins. Any damage to property will be charged to the member.
07
Entry TimingsEntry is not permitted 30 minutes before closing time. Late-sitting requests must be approved by the librarian in advance.
08
Dress CodeDecent and appropriate clothing is required. Management reserves the right to deny entry to inappropriately dressed individuals.
