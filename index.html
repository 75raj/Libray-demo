Library Policy

Rules &
Regulations

Please follow these rules to maintain a peaceful environment for all students at Budha Library.

01
Complete SilenceTalking, whispering, and phone calls are strictly prohibited inside study halls. Use mobile phones only in designated areas outside.
02
No Food InsideEating inside study halls is not allowed. Use the canteen area for snacks and meals. Water bottles are permitted at your desk.
03
Separate ZonesBoys and girls have dedicated separate seating zones. Crossing into the opposite zone is strictly not permitted for safety and comfort of all students.
05
Electronic DevicesLaptops, tablets, and headphones are allowed. Device sound must be muted. Earphones must be used at low volume. Sharing/charging others' devices at your seat is not allowed.
06
CleanlinessKeep your desk and surroundings clean. Dispose of waste in dustbins only. Any damage to library property will be charged to the responsible member.
07
Seat ReservationYou cannot hold seats for absent friends. Unoccupied reserved seats will be released after 30 minutes. Seat swapping is not allowed without staff permission.
08
Discussion Room RulesDiscussion room must be booked in advance. Group discussions allowed here only â€” not in study halls. Maximum 8 students per slot. Time limit: 2 hours.
